wpCssFramework

A tiny footprint CSS framework, that I use for commercial projects to speed up development work. wpCssFramework is a WordPress Plugin, a mish-mash of styles that I most commonly use, mostly my creations, but also from bootstrap and foundation. The goal is to only use minimal Bootstrap classes (columns).

Columns

Stripped back the BS Columns to those I most commonly use, i.e. .col-4 .col-6 .col-8 .col-12, also includes the BS sizes .col-sm-{} .col-md-{} .col-lg-{} .col-xl-{}2
